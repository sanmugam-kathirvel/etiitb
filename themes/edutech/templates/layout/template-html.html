EdTech @IITB

Welcome to the Interdisciplinary Programme in Educational Technology at IIT Bombay. Educational Technology is a combination of processes, strategies, and tools involved in addressing educational needs and problems, with an emphasis on applying current technological tools. We are actively involved in research and teaching areas of educational technology.

The thrust areas of research in the IDP-EdTech are:

Technology Enhanced Learning of
Thinking Skills
(TELoTS)
Teacher Use of
Educational Technology
(TUET)
Educational
Data Analytics
(EDA)
Exploration of Emerging Technologies
for Education
(EmergE)
